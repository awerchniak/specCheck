How to use the spec check program:

A. automatic entry
1. export your full spec book pdf as a .txt file, and save it with a one word name in your "Program" folder
2. double click "specCheck.sh", and bam, you're golden. follow instructions
3. open the "Program" folder and find your finished spec Check

B. manual entry
1. export your index as a .txt file, and save it with a one word name in your "Program" folder
2. export each section as a .txt file, and save it named ######.txt (e.g. save "09 91 00 Painting" as "099100") in your "Program" folder
3. double click "specCheck.sh", and bam, you're golden. follow instructions
4. open the "Program" folder and find your finished spec Check
